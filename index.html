WHO
I AM

Atia Farha

I'm Atia Farha, a passionate undergraduate CSE student from Bangladesh. My interest in programming began in middle school, but in 2022, when I was in class 10, I truly started learning. Since then, I have been gradually developing my programming skills as a hobby alongside my academics. I enjoy development because it never feels tiring to me.

So far, I have worked on many real-world SaaS and collaborative projects. I believe that every pixel has intent, and every function has a story. My top priorities are always user experience, smoothness and performance.

When I'm not knee-deep in code, you might find me solving a Rubik's Cube, playing online games or badminton, listening to music, reading webtoons, watching Korean series, doodling, or searching for random things on Google.
